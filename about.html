A Little Birdy Told Me

Birder's Eye View was founded in 2024 by Maleah, the club president, after she was diagnosed with ADHD and anxiety and took up bird-watching as a form of mindfulness. However, when she tried to find bird-watching groups in San Antonio, she realized that most people were not in her demographic as a 22-year-old half-Filipino and Italian woman. So, she created Birder's Eye View to provide a space where young adults can try a new hobby, relax, and make new friends. We hope to see you around!
